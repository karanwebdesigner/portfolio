Project Overview

A seamless car buying and selling experience.

AutoScout24 is one of Europe’s largest automotive marketplaces. Despite high traffic, users faced friction in discovering trustworthy listings and communicating efficiently.


The opportunity: redesign the mobile experience to build trust, simplify interactions, and streamline the end-to-end car trading journey.

My Role UI Designer
Platform AutoScout24 Mobile
Timeline ~6 Weeks
Outcome +25% increase in listing-to-contact conversion rate
The Problem

Traffic was high. Trust was low.

Users browsed listings frequently but hesitated to take meaningful actions like contacting sellers or posting their own ads.

Signal 01
Low Conversion Intent
Users explored multiple listings but dropped off before initiating conversations or inquiries.
Signal 02
Trust & Communication Gap
Lack of clear verification and inefficient messaging led users to move off-platform or abandon the process.
Growth Strategy

Trust and speed in car trading.

Pillar 01

Increase clarity and trust

Redesigned listings with verified signals, price insights, and simplified information hierarchy reducing uncertainty and encouraging faster user decisions.

Pillar 02

From browsing to transacting

Enabled seamless in-app communication and action-oriented flows, turning the app into a complete car trading platform rather than just a discovery tool.

Solution

From Discovery to Deal

A streamlined mobile experience that builds trust and removes friction at every step, combining verified listings, clear information, and seamless in-app communication to help users confidently move from browsing to completing car transactions.

Impact

Stronger trust and faster transactions.
