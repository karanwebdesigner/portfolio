Overview

A modern discovery experience for RV buyers.

The website struggled with an outdated design and poor usability, making it difficult for users to find relevant vehicles. The opportunity: redesign the website to create a visually engaging, easy-to-navigate experience that simplifies RV discovery and increases lead generation.

My Role Lead Designer
Domain Automotive / RV Marketplace
Timeline ~3–4 Weeks
Team Design / SEO / Growth
Search and discovery interface supporting higher user intent and conversion.
The Problem

Traffic existed. Conversion didn’t.

Users visited the website but struggled to find relevant RVs due to poor navigation and lack of filtering options.

↘

Low Conversion

Visitors browsing without taking action

◎

Poor Discoverability

Users struggling to find relevant RVs

⚠

Drop-off Risk

Early exits due to confusing navigation and lack of filters

Growth Strategy

Simplifying discovery and decision-making.

Pillar 01

Improve search & filtering

Introduced clear categories and smart filters, enabling users to quickly find RVs that match their needs.

Pillar 02

Enhance visual clarity

Redesigned the interface with a modern, structured layout to improve trust and usability.

Final Solution

Effortless RV Discovery

A clean, modern website experience designed to help users easily explore, filter, and find the right RV with clear categories, intuitive navigation, and a simplified browsing flow.

Solution 01
Clear RV categories based on user needs
Solution 02
Advanced filters for precise search
Solution 03
Simplified navigation and browsing flow
Solution 04
Interactive prototyping for stakeholder validation
