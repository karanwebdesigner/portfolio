Project Overview

A unified investing and money management experience.

Questrade is a leading Canadian trading platform. While feature-rich, the mobile experience felt fragmented users struggled to seamlessly manage investments, track portfolios, and handle finances in one place.


The opportunity: redesign the mobile app to simplify financial decision-making while retaining powerful tools for all user levels.

My Role Product Designer
Platform Questrade Mobile
Timeline ~4 Weeks
Outcome +18% increase in daily active usage
The Problem

High Engagement. Low Clarity.

Users were actively checking markets but hesitated when it came to making confident financial decisions like executing trades or managing portfolios.

Signal 01
Fragmented Experience
Users switched between multiple tools for trading, tracking, and budgeting, breaking their workflow.
Signal 02
Decision Friction
Complex interfaces and scattered data made users delay or avoid taking action, especially on mobile.
Growth Strategy

Confidence driven investing on mobile.

Pillar 01

Reduce decision friction

Simplified complex financial data into clear, actionable insights helping users move from analysis to action faster and with more confidence.

Pillar 02

From tracking tool to decision engine

Introduced personalized dashboards, smart alerts, and guided actions that transformed the app into a proactive investing companion, not just a passive portfolio viewer.

Impact

Measurable growth in user confidence and activity.

+19%
Trade execution rate
+27%
Daily active users engaging with portfolio insights
↓31%

"The app evolved from a monitoring tool into a confident decision-making platform."
